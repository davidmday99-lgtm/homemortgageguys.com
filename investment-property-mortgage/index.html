Investment Property Mortgage

Mortgage guidance for rental and investment property buyers.

Investment property financing usually depends on both borrower strength and the details of the property, including expected rental use, reserves, and documentation.

Apply Now Call David

Understand the numbers behind the property

Investment loans can involve different down payment, reserve, and underwriting considerations than a primary residence. It helps to review the full picture before writing an offer.

David can help you organize the basic numbers, talk through documentation, and compare possible financing paths for qualified investment purchases.

What to talk through early

  • Property type and intended rental use
  • Down payment and reserve planning
  • Current debts and income documentation
  • Cash flow expectations and timeline

Do investment properties require different documentation?

Often, yes. Documentation can vary by loan program, property type, borrower profile, and whether rental income is used.

Should I talk to a loan advisor before making an offer?

Yes. Investment property financing can move more smoothly when reserves, property details, and documentation are reviewed early.
