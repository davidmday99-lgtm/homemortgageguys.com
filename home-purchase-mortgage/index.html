Prepare for a cleaner home purchase process

A home purchase mortgage starts with the full picture: income, assets, credit, debts, property goals, and timing. Looking at those pieces early can help you avoid surprises once you are under contract.

David can help you compare available loan paths, estimate documentation needs, and understand how the mortgage process may affect your home search.

What to talk through early

  • Pre-approval timing before touring homes
  • Income and asset documents that may be requested
  • Estimated payment comfort and cash-to-close planning
  • Property type, occupancy, and closing timeline

When should I talk to a loan advisor?

Before you make an offer is usually best. Early review gives you time to understand documents, budget, and potential loan options.

Is a pre-approval the same as final approval?

No. Final approval depends on underwriting, property review, program guidelines, and updated documentation.
